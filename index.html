REPARATIONS: THE BREAKDOWN

It's the act of making amends for a wrong that was done. Think of it like trying to repair a deep and lasting harm.

1. THE HARM (e.g., Stolen Land)
2. THE REPAIR (Actions to fix it)

Forms of Repair Can Include:

Money

Payments

Land

Returning territories

Programs

New housing/education

Apology

Admitting wrong

VINDICATE: THE BREAKDOWN

To clear someone of blame, or to prove that an action (or idea) was right, reasonable, or justified all along.

1. AN ACTION OR IDEA (e.g., "Taking this land was necessary")
2. PROVEN "RIGHT" (New info or argument makes it seem justified)

So, the question is asking: Can reparations make colonization seem "okay" or "justified" after the fact?

COLONIZATION: THE BREAKDOWN

The process where one country establishes control over another area or people, often for economic gain.

1. POWERFUL NATION
2. TAKES CONTROL OF
3. ANOTHER REGION

Forms of Control Include:

Settlers

Moving its own people in

Government

Imposing new laws

Economy

Taking resources

Culture

Changing beliefs/language

MUNSEE LENAPE: THE BREAKDOWN

The original inhabitants of Manhattan Island. They are a specific band (or group) of the larger Lenape people, native to the New York City area.

WHO: A specific Native American group
WHERE: Manhattan & surrounding area

ALGONQUIN: THE BREAKDOWN

A large family of Native American tribes linked by similar languages. The Munsee Lenape are part of this group. Think of it like a "language family tree."

WHAT: A large language family
INCLUDES: Lenape, Ojibwe, Cree, & many others

DOCTRINE OF DISCOVERY: THE BREAKDOWN

A 15th-century European legal and religious concept that said Christian explorers could claim any "non-Christian" land they "discovered."

1. EUROPEAN SHIP "DISCOVERS" LAND
2. ARE PEOPLE CHRISTIAN? (NO)
3. LAND CAN BE LEGALLY CLAIMED

This was used to justify seizing land from Indigenous peoples worldwide.

MANIFEST DESTINY: THE BREAKDOWN

A 19th-century belief that American settlers were "destined" by God to expand and control all of North America, from the Atlantic to the Pacific.

THE BELIEF: "It is our God-given destiny..." THE ACTION: "...to expand West and take the entire continent."

URBANIZATION: THE BREAKDOWN

The process of a population shifting from living in rural (country) areas to living in urban (city) areas.

FROM: RURAL (Country / Reservations)
TO: URBAN (Cities)

This question asks if this shift (part of a 1950s gov policy) was actually good for them.

INDIANS OF ALL TRIBES: THE BREAKDOWN

A group of Native American activists, many of them college students, who came together from many different tribes to protest for Indigenous rights.

WHO: Activists from many tribes
WHAT: Protest group (occupied Alcatraz)

They famously occupied Alcatraz Island from 1969 to 1971.
